Welcome to DCI Big 6!

The world of Drum Corps International is large and wide, and it can be difficult to find everything you need to stay up to date. That's why this website exists - it's the place that pulls everything Drum Crop into one, easily accessible place for unparalleled ease. We feature everything from corps history and recent information, to score breakdowns from Drum Corps Shows past. If you ever have any questions, you can also contact us using the contact page in the navigation menu for any specific questions. Still, hopefully, this site can serve as a large enough reserve of information for that to be solved as soon as you land here.
At the top, you will find menu tabs for what we consider to be the top six drum corps in active competition today: The Blue Devils, the Bluecoats, the Cadets, Carolina Crown, the Cavaliers, and Santa Clara Vanguard. These corps represent a large portion of DCI history, and we hope to widen everyone's understanding and appreciation of drum corps through the retelling and analysis of the history of these individual corps. This goes without saying, but focusing on the "top six" corps does not discount in any way the tremendous impact of all the other corps - both active and inactive - that have molded drum corps into what it is today, and what it will be in the future. There is also a page dedicated to historical World Championship scores for those of you who enjoy number crunching and analyzing numerical trends in a competitive nature.
With that said, prepare to immerse yourself in the robust world that is Drum Corps International, enjoy!
